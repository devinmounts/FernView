Live Actively at FernView Farm

While at FernView Farm, enjoy different activies by oneself or with community members (ranging from outdoor activities to group contributions). We hope that you will enjoy your stay and that you will actively add to the life and well-being of the community.

Examples of activies at FernView Farm include:

  • Swimming
  • Childcare
  • Plant Identification
  • Foraging
  • Collaborative Skill-Building
  • Animal Husbandry
  • Production of home goods
  • Relaxing
